Museum Robbing Simulator

About

Museum robbing simulator is a first person action game, where you try to steal valuables before the timer expires, while defending against guards.
The game was made during the Xamk Game Jam 2 2021
I was the programmer for this project. While all of us took part in designing the game, i did the player movement, pickup, and throw. I also set the final scene.
