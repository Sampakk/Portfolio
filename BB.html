Basement Breakout

About

Basement Breakout is a PS1 style horror escaperoom game where you must solve puzzles and get out.
This game was made during the Xamk Game Jam 1 2022.
I was the programmer for this project. While all of us took part in designing the game, i did the player movement, item pickup, object interaction, HUD, and few puzzles
